Amish Slaw

Description

Amish Slaw is a type of cole slaw--a side dish consisting of shredded cabbage--typically excluding the mayonnaise that traditional cole slaws have.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp celery seeds
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1tsp mustard
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, toss cabbage, onion,vinegar, and 1 cup of sugar together
  2. In a small saucepan, combine salt, celery seeds, 1tsp sugar, mustard and oil
  3. Bring to a boil and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Let cool then pour over cabbage mixture
  5. Toss to coat
  6. Refrigerate overnight
